📐 Resolution & Aspect Ratio

Analysis of image dimensions and aspect ratio distributions

Key Findings

  • Standard Resolutions: Most images cluster around 640×427 and 640×480 pixels
  • Aspect Ratios: Predominantly landscape (4:3) and wide-screen (16:9) formats
  • Consistency: Careful preprocessing ensures uniform image dimensions
  • Diversity: Minor variations accommodate natural photography differences
💡 Insight: Standard resolutions simplify preprocessing and model training. The consistency in dimensions indicates professional dataset curation.

Brightness Analysis

Images show diverse brightness levels reflecting natural photography conditions:

  • Very Dark: Low-light indoor scenes and nighttime photography
  • Dark: Shaded outdoor areas and subdued indoor lighting
  • Medium: Well-lit indoor spaces and overcast outdoor scenes
  • Bright: Bright indoor lighting and sunny outdoor conditions
  • Very Bright: Harsh sunlight and high-contrast lighting

🔬 Advanced Visual Analysis

Detailed visual and textural properties

📈 Edge Density

Measure of image complexity based on edge pixels. High edge density indicates complex scenes with multiple objects or detailed textures.

🎯 Contrast Ratio

Difference between bright and dark areas. High contrast images are typically easier for human and machine perception.

🌈 Color Saturation

Intensity of colors in images. Reflects whether images are captured under natural or artificial lighting and color processing.

✨ Sharpness

Focus quality and detail clarity. Essential for object recognition and fine-grained visual understanding tasks.
